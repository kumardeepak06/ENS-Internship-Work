Here's how your hair loss will advance if you don't treat it

Age: 24 Yrs

Stage 3v

Wider forehead from a receded hairline, scalp starts becoming visible on the crown area.

Age: 25 Yrs

Stage 4

Hair loss has significantly advanced & balding starts to occur on the crown area

Age: 26 Yrs

Stage 5

Hair loss is severe, there's a small strip of hair between the hairline & the balding crown

Age: 27 Yrs

Stage 6

Balding has advanced to the front and top. Hairline has receded completely.

Age: 28 Yrs

Stage 7

There's no hair present on the top & front of the head. The only hair is present around the sides of the head.
